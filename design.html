Web Design

Customized Wordpress website for a family-owned antique business in Santa Barbara. The landing page header reminds customers of the iconic storefront on State Street. The scrolling, single-page design promotes a feeling of continuous exploration through the Antique Alley.

Document Redesign

Poster redesign for a film screening schedule at UCSB and invite redesign for a symposium at UCLA. The goal for both projects was to aim for cleaner visuals and a brighter tone while maintaining the key elements.
